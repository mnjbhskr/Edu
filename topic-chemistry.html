⚗️Chemistry

The mathematics of molecules and reactions

Chemistry is built on mathematical foundations — from quantum mechanical descriptions of electron orbitals to the kinetics of chemical reactions and the thermodynamics of phase transitions.

This topic family reveals the deep mathematical structures that govern the molecular world. You will explore how symmetry groups classify molecules, how rate laws and activation energies determine reaction speed, how atomic and molecular orbitals arise from solving the Schrödinger equation, and how Gibbs free energy dictates which reactions proceed spontaneously.

From the oscillating colours of the Belousov-Zhabotinsky reaction to the spectral fingerprints that identify unknown compounds, mathematics is the language in which chemistry is written.
