This is where mathematics reveals its true power. Calculus, differential equations, vector methods, and Fourier analysis are the languages of physics, engineering, and finance. You're no longer just solving problems—you're understanding the continuous and discrete worlds at their deepest levels.

From limits and derivatives to eigenvalues and Markov chains, you'll explore the machinery that drives modern science. These are the ideas that separate mathematical literacy from mathematical mastery.
