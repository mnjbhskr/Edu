2. Permitted Educational Use

Visitors to MathsEdu.org may engage with our materials in the following ways:

When using our content, proper attribution should be given. Please cite our work as: Source: MathsEdu.org

When referencing MathsEdu.org materials in academic papers, essays, or other scholarly work, please cite the page URL and include proper attribution. A recommended citation format is:
