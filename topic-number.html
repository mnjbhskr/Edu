ℕNumber Theory

Exploring the nature of integers, primes, sequences, and infinite cardinality

Number theory is one of the oldest branches of mathematics, concerned with the properties and relationships of integers. From the prime numbers that underpin modern cryptography to the elegant patterns found in sequences and series, number theory reveals the deep structure underlying all mathematics.

This topic family explores the fundamental concepts: how numbers relate to one another, what makes primes special, how patterns emerge from counting, and what infinity truly means. You'll encounter both classical results (dating back millennia) and modern applications that power the digital world.

Whether you're interested in pure mathematical beauty or practical applications in computer science, number theory provides essential foundations for advanced study across all mathematical disciplines.
