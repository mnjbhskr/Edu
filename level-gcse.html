Core Mathematics

You've grasped the fundamentals. Now deepen your understanding. At this level, mathematics becomes more sophisticated and more powerful. You'll tackle complex numbers, prove theorems rigorously, and apply probability in realistic scenarios.

From the binomial theorem to modular arithmetic, from the unit circle to conic sections—these are the tools that connect abstract theory to practical application. Mastery here prepares you for advanced mathematics and beyond.
