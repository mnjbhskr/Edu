Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information Collected

MathsEdu may collect limited information through:

  • Website analytics — this may include pages visited, time spent on pages, general geographic region, browser type and device. This information is collected in aggregate form and is used solely to understand how the site is used and to improve the educational experience.
  • Voluntary contact — if you contact us via email, we may store your email address and the content of your enquiry.

2. Cookies

The site may use limited cookies for basic analytics and site performance. These cookies do not identify individual users.

3. Use of Information

Information collected is used solely to:

  • understand how visitors use the website,
  • improve educational content, and
  • respond to enquiries.

MathsEdu does not sell or share personal data with third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect information held by the site. However, no internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

5. Third-Party Services

If analytics services or embedded content are used, those services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

MathsEdu is designed for educational use by students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Your Rights (UK / GDPR)

Under UK data protection law, individuals have rights including:

  • access to personal data,
  • correction of inaccurate information, and
  • request for deletion where appropriate.

Requests may be directed to:

privacy@mathsedu.org

8. Changes to this Policy

This policy may be updated periodically.
