2 · Frisch-Waugh-Lovell Theorem

The coefficient on X₁ in a multiple regression equals the coefficient from regressing the residuals of Y on X₂ against the residuals of X₁ on X₂. This is "holding X₂ constant".

Select a regressor above.
The Frisch-Waugh theorem explains what "controlling for" means in regression: it is equivalent to first removing the influence of other variables, then examining what remains.
