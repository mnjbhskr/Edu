Simple Linear Regression

Drag a line to minimise residuals, then let OLS find the optimum

1 · Drag the Line

Click and drag on the canvas to adjust the line. The sum of squared residuals (SSR) updates in real time. Press Solve OLS to snap to the best fit.

SSR: —
The OLS estimator minimises the sum of squared vertical distances from each point to the fitted line. Try dragging to beat OLS — you cannot!
