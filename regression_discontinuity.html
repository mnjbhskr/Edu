2. Fuzzy RD

Below the cutoff, compliance is imperfect. Some units receive treatment even below the threshold, and some do not receive it above. The fuzzy RD estimate equals the ratio of the reduced-form jump to the first-stage jump — an instrumental variables estimator.

10%
80%
Fuzzy RD = IV: The fuzzy RD estimator is numerically identical to a two-stage least squares estimator using 1(X ≥ c) as an instrument for treatment. The first stage is the jump in treatment probability; the reduced form is the jump in the outcome.

3. McCrary Density Test

If individuals can precisely manipulate the running variable, the density will show bunching at the cutoff. This invalidates the RD design. Toggle manipulation to see the effect.
