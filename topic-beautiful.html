★Beautiful Results

The crown jewels of mathematics

The most celebrated theorems and surprising connections in mathematics — results that reveal deep, unexpected harmony. These are the moments when disparate areas of mathematics suddenly converge, producing equations and structures of breathtaking elegance.

From Euler’s identity, which ties together five fundamental constants in a single equation, to the Mandelbrot set, which generates infinite complexity from the simplest possible iteration, these results have inspired generations of mathematicians.

Some topics here are accessible to anyone with curiosity; others require years of preparation. Together they represent what many consider the greatest achievements of human mathematical thought.

★ Start Here
Euler’s Identity (eiπ + 1 = 0)

Five constants, three operations, one equation — widely regarded as the most beautiful formula in all of mathematics.
